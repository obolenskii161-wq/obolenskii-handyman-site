About Konstantin

I have over 15 years of construction experience — both in the United States and internationally. I started as a facade installer and spent years working hands-on in exterior systems.

After 10 years in the field, I built and managed my own construction and advertising company with 8 working crews. Today I bring that same structure, discipline, and quality control to residential projects here in Sacramento.
